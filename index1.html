Beach Mule

The beach should be a fun and relaxing experience. However, there is a lot of hassle when it comes to visiting the beach. This is where our solution comes in, Beach Mule. Beach Mule is no ordinary beach cart. It is an innovative and advanced beach cart.

Beach Mule pairs a strong motor with a smart sensor that tracks the user. It follows the user through a GPS module in the customers phone, which allows the user to carry all of their heavy belongings to the beach hands-free (Supports IOS 7+). It has a weight capacity of 120lbs, which is enough to support coolers, chairs, and many other beach items. Its strong motor allows for it to reach speeds of up to 3.5mph, which makes sure that the Beach Mule doesn't fall far behind the user.
